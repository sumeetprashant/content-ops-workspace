## Executive Summary

ICME (Institute for Continuing Medical Education) is a 22+ year old medical exam coaching institute based in Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum), Kerala, led by Dr. Suresh Prabhakar. They train doctors for international medical licensing exams (PLAB, AMC, MRCP, MRCS, NZREX, USMLE, FMGE). ICME operates primarily as an offline, classroom-intensive training center with minimal digital content presence -- representing a significant opportunity for Content Studio to help them scale through professional video production and digital content.

**Opportunity Rating:** MEDIUM -- Strong content need, but budget signals are modest (small institute, low-cost pricing model). Best approached with a targeted pilot project.

## 6. Content Studio Angle

### Where CS Adds Most Value

1. **Lecture Recording Series** -- ICME's biggest asset is Dr. Suresh Prabhakar's 25+ years of teaching expertise. Recording a structured lecture series would convert this institutional knowledge into a scalable digital product. Content Studio's multi-camera setup and acoustic isolation are perfectly suited for this.

2. **Student Testimonial Videos** -- ICME has strong text testimonials from students who passed PLAB/AMC with high scores. Converting these to professionally produced video testimonials would dramatically boost credibility and social proof.

3. **Campus/Facility Showcase** -- Professional video tours of the Trivandrum facility and the new Jaipur center for marketing purposes.

### Best Entry Point Service

**Recommended entry:** A 2-3 day "Digital Content Starter Pack" shoot:
- 5-10 short student testimonial videos (reels/shorts format)
- 1 campus tour video
- 1 "Meet the Director" brand film featuring Dr. Suresh Prabhakar
- Social media content package (30-60 second clips)

**Pricing:** Rs. 40,000-60,000 (positioned as a pilot to demonstrate ROI)
**Rationale:** Low-commitment entry point that delivers immediate, visible results for social media. Avoids the bigger commitment of a full lecture series until trust is established.

### AI Upsell Opportunity (CS Labs)

- **AI Voice Enhancement:** Relevant if ICME begins recording lectures -- classroom audio in Trivandrum may have ambient noise issues that AI can clean up.
- **AI Content Generation:** Could help ICME generate social media content, blog posts, and study material from recorded lectures (lecture-to-notes pipeline).
- **AI Video Enhancement:** Relevant for repurposing any existing low-quality video content into professional-grade material.

**AI Upsell Timing:** Phase 2, after the initial content pilot demonstrates value.
